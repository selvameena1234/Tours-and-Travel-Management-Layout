    Day-by-Day Itinerary




    Day 1: Arrival in Delhi

    Pick-up from Delhi airport/railway station

    Check-in to hotel

    Visit: India Gate, Lotus Temple, Qutub Minar, Humayun’s Tomb

    Drive past Parliament House and Rashtrapati Bhavan

    Overnight stay in Delhi







    Day 2: Delhi Sightseeing – Agra (230 km / 4 hrs)

    Morning visit to Jama Masjid and Chandni Chowk (optional rickshaw ride)

    Drive to Agra

    Visit Mehtab Bagh for sunset view of Taj Mahal

    Overnight stay in Agra



    Day 3: Agra Sightseeing – Jaipur (240 km / 5 hrs)



    Early morning visit to Taj Mahal (sunrise view)

    Breakfast at hotel

    Visit Agra Fort and Tomb of Itimad-ud-Daulah (“Baby Taj”)

    Drive to Jaipur via Fatehpur Sikri (UNESCO site)

    Check-in and overnight stay in Jaipur





    Day 4: Jaipur Sightseeing



    • Visit Amber Fort (elephant or jeep ride to fort)
    • See Hawa Mahal, City Palace, and Jantar Mantar
    • Explore local markets for handicrafts and textiles
    • Optional: Dinner at Chokhi Dhani (cultural village)
    • Overnight stay in Jaipur


    Day 5: Jaipur – Delhi (280 km / 5–6 hrs)



    • Leisurely breakfast
    • Drive back to Delhi
    • Optional stops at roadside dhabas and cultural points
    • Overnight stay in Delhi or transfer to airport/train station




    Day 6: Departure from Delhi

    • Morning at leisure or local market visit
    • Airport/railway station drop for onward journey
